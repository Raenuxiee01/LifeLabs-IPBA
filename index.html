Fundraising Proposal for Sitio Bebe

A small town is currently facing a severe health crisis. This town is not close to a natural waterway so the local government often imports water clean water for residents, or opening wells. Because of this, there is often a drought in the area especially in summer. This community needs help. As Filipinos who care about others, we must act to alleviate, even a little, what our fellow Filipinos are suffering in Sitio Bebe. The local government alone cannot handle this as our government's funds have already gone to responding to the pandemic. As event organizers, let's help Sitio Bebe.
