Frequently Asked Questions

What is wellness?

Wellness is a holistic integration of physical, mental, and spiritual well-being. It's an active pursuit of activities, choices, and lifestyles that lead to a state of good health and high levels of well-being and life satisfaction. Wellness is not just the absence of disease, but a proactive approach to living a healthy and fulfilling life. It encompasses various dimensions, including physical, emotional, mental, social, and spiritual well-being, and is often achieved through a combination of self-care practices, healthy habits, and a supportive environment.

How can I improve my wellness?

You can improve your wellness by focusing on physical, mental, and emotional health through various practices.
